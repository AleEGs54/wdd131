Site Plan

Site Name

Title: The Game Glimpse

Site Purpose

The site provides a place where gamers have a voice about the games the like, play and enjoy. People can fill a form to submit an opinion about a game. It also allows other to decide wheter to try/buy a video game or not.

Scenarios

  • What is the most rated video game at the moment?
  • What does experienced gamers say about this particular video game?
